Hi, I'm David Carnell
"A highschool teacher with a passion for driving"

I first started driving in around 1996 on my parents 'farm' long before I was old enough to drive on the road. When I was old enough though, I successfully passed my manual driving test in 2003 with the assistance of a good driving instructor. Then in 2007 I had the opportunity to start driving heavy vehicles professionally in a part-time capacity, so with the help of another good driving instructor I passed my HR Truck driving test and continued driving part-time even after I started teaching in 2009. Since then, I have had the opportunity to drive a large variety of vehicles both professionally and privately in many off-road and on-road situations, whilst also teaching a many different subjects, across all year levels, and in a variety of locations.

I enjoy driving and I enjoy teaching and in early 2018, I had a light-bulb moment, and after consultation with my wife, decided to combine the two and start Carnell Car School. It began with just driving lessons, and in a partnership with parents and other supervisors, over the years I have guided countless students to the joy and independence of being driver's licence holders. However, not long into my driving instructor career, it became apparent that there was a lot more knowledge and there were a lot more skills surrounding driving that learners and the general public could gain that would make them safer vehicle operators. With that revelation, a plan was put in place to offer Offroad Driver Training, Skid Pan Experiences and Basic Maintenance Courses, at a reasonable price, under the Carnell Car School umbrella.

It took some time, and some false starts, but I am proud to say that Carnell Car School now offers all of the above courses, in addition to the very successful, individually-focused, manual driving lessons that have been the back-bone of the Carnell Car School business for many years.

This compact SUV is the perfect car in which to learn to drive. With a high driving position and great visibility you will find it easy to see potential hazards on the roads. The short wheel-base and small turning circle make all those technical maneuvers a breeze to learn and easy to repeat time and time again.

With a slightly lower-than-standard gear ratio and an engine designed to produce lots of torque low in the rev-range, the Jimny is less likely to stall than most other small vehicles on the roads today. This all contributes to a simpler, easier and more straight-forward driving experience with Carnell Car School.

This late model Jimny has a manual gearbox, fitted with bar-type instructor pedals (Dual Controls Queensland) and a full set of instructor mirrors.

Automatic or Manual lessons in your own car are by negotiation. Please contact David to talk through the requirements if you wish to take lessons in your own vehicle.

Basic Maintenance Course
$150
per person
The Basic Maintenance Course is a 3 hour practical course that is presented to small groups. This course will help you take care of your vehicle more comprehensively and will include the following: a brief overview of vehicle parts and function, a brief overview of some things to look for when purchasing used cars, refueling a vehicle, checking tread levels on tyre, changing a tyre, checking and adjusting tyre pressures, checking and topping up fluid levels, checking and changing fuses, checking and change light bulbs, changing a battery. There will be a Carnell Car School vehicle onsite as an example or if participant doesn't have access to their own vehicle, but it is recommended that participants bring and learn on their own vehicle as every vehicle is unique.

Offroad Driving Course
$120
per vehicle
The Offroad Driving Course is 3 hour course undertaken in a small group setting at the RACQ Mobility Centre at Mt Cotton/Cornubia using their purpose-built 4WD training facility. Find out what you and your vehicle are capable of, with water crossings; articulation tests; steep inclines and declines; ramp over, approach and departure angle testing; tight, windy, low-traction trails; and much more. Starting out with a walk-through, talk-through of basic trails and finishing off with a self-directed exploration of any and all available obstacle and/or trail within the 4WD facility, there is something for everyone in this course.

Skid Pan Experience
$200
per person
The Skid Pan Experience is a 3 hour window of opportunity to put your vehicle through it's paces in a controlled, low-grip environment, without the danger of other road users. Learn how it feels to lose control of your vehicle due to lack of grip. Learn how to recognise and control a slide and how to avoid losing control in wet and slippery conditions. Discuss your new-found knowledge and skills with other course participants while you wait for your next turn on the slippery stuff. At the end of the course take those skills with you and apply them in the real world to help keep yourself and other road-users safer when road conditions are less than ideal. Please note, that there is a wash-down facility on-site to ensure that the slipping and sliding doesn't unexpectedly follow you onto the public road at the end of the event.
